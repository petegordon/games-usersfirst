About Users First Games

Users First Games is an indie game studio based in Columbus, Ohio. We believe the best games put players at the center of every decision — from accessible controls to inclusive design.

Our debut title, Tandemonium, is a physics-based tandem bicycle party game built for cooperative chaos. It started as a passion project and grew into a full multiplayer experience playable across web, mobile, and desktop.

We're proud members of The Central Ohio GameDev Group (COGG), GDG Columbus, and CBUS Code & Coffee — Columbus's communities for game developers, tech builders, and creators of all levels.

Players & Users Come First

It's the name and the mission. Every decision starts with the experience you have — from intuitive controls to seamless multiplayer. If it doesn't make the game better for you, we don't ship it.

Fun Is Better Together

The best moments in gaming happen with other people. We build games around relationships, cooperation, and shared laughter — because a tandem bike doesn't ride itself.

Open Source, Open Doors

We believe in building in the open. Our code is available to learn from, build on, and contribute to. Great games grow when everyone can see how they work.

Embrace the Chaos

Physics-driven, not scripted. Every ride is different. The wobbles and crashes aren't bugs — they're the stories you'll tell your friends about tomorrow.
