About Users First Games

Users First Games is an indie game studio based in Columbus, Ohio. We believe the best games put players at the center of every decision — from accessible controls to inclusive design.

Our debut title, Tandemonium, is a physics-based tandem bicycle party game built for cooperative chaos. It started as a passion project and grew into a full multiplayer experience playable across web, mobile, and desktop.

We're proud members of The Central Ohio GameDev Group (COGG), Columbus's community for game developers of all levels.

What We Believe

Players Come First

It's in our name. Every feature, every design choice, every line of code starts with the player experience.

Accessibility Matters

Games should be playable by everyone. We build with multiple input methods, safety modes, and thoughtful defaults.

Play Together

The best gaming moments happen with friends. We design for cooperation, laughter, and shared experiences.

Open & Community-Driven

We're part of the Columbus game dev community and believe in building games in the open, with player feedback guiding the way.
